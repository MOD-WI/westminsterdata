⚠️ Critical Methodological Note: Survival Analysis

Nearly 50% of customers are still active (right-censored data). Using simple averages severely underestimates customer lifetime because it treats active customers as if they'll churn immediately. Kaplan-Meier survival analysis properly accounts for these ongoing relationships.
