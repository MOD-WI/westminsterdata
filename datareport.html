Executive Summary

This analysis of 29,694 customers across 742 business practices reveals distinct customer segments with varying lifetime values and tenure patterns.

There is an inverse relationship between revenue per policy and customer tenure: medical practices generate high revenue (£140–160/policy) but shorter tenure (~2 years), while professional/consulting practices offer lower revenue (£50–70/policy) but longer tenure (~6 years).

Critical Methodological Note: Survival Analysis

Nearly 50% of customers are still active (right-censored data). Simple averages underestimate customer lifetime by treating active customers as if they churn immediately. Kaplan-Meier properly accounts for these ongoing relationships.
